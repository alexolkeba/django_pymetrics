Progressive Planning Challenge

The Goal: Your objective is to solve a series of puzzles by moving a stack of disks to a new tower.

How to Play:

  • Move the entire stack of disks from the starting tower to the destination tower (the rightmost one).
  • Click a tower to select the top disk, then click another tower to move it.

The Rules:

  • You can only move one disk at a time.
  • A larger disk can never be placed on top of a smaller disk.

The Challenge: The puzzles will become more complex as you succeed. Aim for the fewest moves possible. Think ahead!

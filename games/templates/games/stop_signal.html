Inhibitory Control Challenge

This task measures your inhibitory control—your brain's ability to hit the brakes on a planned action. It's a key part of self-control and focus.

How to Play:

  • Primary Task (Go): An arrow will appear. Your main job is to press the corresponding Left or Right arrow key as quickly as you can.
  • The Twist (Stop): On some trials, the arrow will suddenly change color right after it appears. This is your signal to STOP! and withhold your keypress.

The real challenge is to be fast on the 'Go' trials while successfully hitting the brakes on the 'Stop' trials. Good luck!

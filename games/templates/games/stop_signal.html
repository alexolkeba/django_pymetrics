Inhibitory Control Challenge

Your task is to respond to the direction of the arrow as quickly and accurately as possible.

  • Press LEFT (←) for a left-pointing arrow.
  • Press RIGHT (→) for a right-pointing arrow.
  • BUT... if the arrow turns red, you must STOP and NOT press any key.

This task measures your ability to inhibit a response. Good luck!
